Welcome to Monash University Malaysia's Advanced Computing Platform (ACP)

Unlock the power of cutting-edge computational resources and accelerate your research with the Advanced Computing Platform (ACP) — Monash University Malaysia’s premier high-performance computing environment designed to support and fuel groundbreaking discoveries across disciplines.

What is ACP?​

ACP is a robust and scalable High Performance Computing (HPC) infrastructure tailored to meet the demanding needs of today’s data-intensive research. Featuring state-of-the-art CPU and GPU clusters, high-speed networking, and extensive storage solutions, ACP empowers researchers and students to handle complex simulations, large-scale data analytics, artificial intelligence (AI) workflows, and scientific computing with unparalleled efficiency and speed.

What ACP Offers​

  • High-Performance Compute Clusters
    Access to thousands of Intel CPU cores and cutting-edge NVIDIA GPUs optimized for parallel and accelerated computing workloads.
  • Scalable Storage Solutions
    Large-scale, high-throughput parallel file systems ensuring seamless data access and management.
  • User-Friendly Job Scheduling
    SLURM job scheduler that facilitates efficient resource allocation and workload management.
  • Interactive and Notebook Environments
    Integrated JupyterLab and remote visualization tools for flexible, interactive development and analysis.
  • Comprehensive Support and Training
    Dedicated HPC specialists provide guidance, training, and consultation to maximize your research productivity.
