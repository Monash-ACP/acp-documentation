# Welcome to Monash University Malaysia's Advanced Computing Platform (ACP)

<p style={{ fontSize: "1.1rem", lineHeight: 1.6 }}>
  Unlock the power of cutting-edge computational resources and accelerate your
  research with the Advanced Computing Platform (ACP) — Monash University
  Malaysia’s premier high-performance computing environment designed to support
  and fuel groundbreaking discoveries across disciplines.
</p>

## What is ACP?

<p style={{ fontSize: "1.1rem", lineHeight: 1.6 }}>
  ACP is a robust and scalable High Performance Computing (HPC) infrastructure
  tailored to meet the demanding needs of today’s data-intensive research.
  Featuring state-of-the-art CPU and GPU clusters, high-speed networking, and
  extensive storage solutions, ACP empowers researchers and students to handle
  complex simulations, large-scale data analytics, artificial intelligence (AI)
  workflows, and scientific computing with unparalleled efficiency and speed.
</p>

## What ACP Offers

<ul style={{fontSize: '1.1rem', lineHeight: 1.6}}>
  <li><strong>High-Performance Compute Clusters</strong><br />
  Access to thousands of Intel CPU cores and cutting-edge NVIDIA GPUs optimized for parallel and accelerated computing workloads.</li>

<li>
  <strong>Scalable Storage Solutions</strong>
  <br />
  Large-scale, high-throughput parallel file systems ensuring seamless data
  access and management.
</li>

<li>
  <strong>User-Friendly Job Scheduling</strong>
  <br />
  SLURM job scheduler that facilitates efficient resource allocation and
  workload management.
</li>

<li>
  <strong>Interactive and Notebook Environments</strong>
  <br />
  Integrated JupyterLab and remote visualization tools for flexible, interactive
  development and analysis.
</li>

  <li><strong>Comprehensive Support and Training</strong><br />
  Dedicated HPC specialists provide guidance, training, and consultation to maximize your research productivity.</li>
</ul>
