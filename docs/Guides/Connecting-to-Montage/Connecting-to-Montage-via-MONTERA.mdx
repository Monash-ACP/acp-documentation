MONTERA is our modern, web-based portal for interacting with the MONTAGE HPC clusters. It provides a seamless interface for managing Slurm jobs and launching interactive computing environments directly from your browser, eliminating the need for complex SSH tunnels or local client configuration.

## Logging in to MONTERA

:::important VPN Requirement
To access MONTERA, you must be connected to the Monash Malaysia Internal Network or **MUMVPN**.
:::

1. **Access**: Open your browser and navigate to: [**http://10.168.151.1:3000**](http://10.168.151.1:3000)
2. **Login**: You will be greeted by a secure login page. Enter your cluster **Username** and **Password**.
3. **Success**: Once authenticated, you will arrive at the HPC Dashboard.
