## Your Account

The top navigation bar allows you to manage your profile and session:

- **HPC ID**: Your username is displayed prominently for reference.
- **Manage Password**: You can change your Cluster password directly from the dashboard.
- **Secure Logout**: Always remember to log out when using shared workstations.

## Cluster Resource Status

Monitor the real-time availability of the Montage cluster:

- **Available CPUs**: Track how many cores are currently idle vs. the total capacity.
- **Available Memory**: View global RAM availability across all compute nodes.
- **Active Nodes**: See how many of the 12 total nodes are currently powering research.
- **Available GPUs**: A high-level view of GPU occupancy, including specialized A100 and H100 cards.

## Active Servers & Workspaces

This section provides a live view of any interactive sessions you currently have running:

- **Service Type**: Identify which app (Jupyter, Desktop, VS Code, etc.) is active.
- **Job ID**: The SLURM job ID associated with the session.
- **Connection Info**: The specific internal host (e.g., `10.168.151.21`) where your job is running.
- **Time Matrix**: Track your remaining session time against the requested limit.
- **Actions**: Use the **Connect** button to jump into your workspace, or **Terminate** to immediately stop the job and free up resources.

## Storage & Quotas

MONTERA provides visual progress bars for your storage allocations:

- **Home Directory**: Monitor your personal 500 GB allocation.
- **Shared Storage**: View the usage and quota for your school or project's shared space (e.g., `/ibm/gpfs/soit`).

:::tip Avoid Disk Full Errors
The dashboard is the best place to check if you are nearing your storage limit before submitting large-scale batch jobs.
:::
