title: Create your HPC ID account
sidebar_position: 1
---

If you don't have an account, request for an ACP account. This account is not the same as the network account or your email account. Follow these steps:

1. Go to this [iLab link](https://mum.ilab.agilent.com/service_center/4628/?tab=services).
1. Sign in using Monash University Malaysia credentials.
    <img src={require('@site/static/img/Guides/Getting-Started/Create-your-HPC-ID-account_1.png').default}
        alt="Create-your-HPC-ID-account_1"
        style={{ width: '100%', maxHeight: '300px', objectFit: 'cover', marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}
    />
1. Sign Up! Click 'request service' on HPC User Registration Request.
    <img src={require('@site/static/img/Guides/Getting-Started/Create-your-HPC-ID-account_2.png').default}
        alt="Create-your-HPC-ID-account_2"
        style={{ width: '100%', maxHeight: '300px', objectFit: 'cover', marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}
    />
1. Fill in the details.
1. Save your completed form.
    <img src={require('@site/static/img/Guides/Getting-Started/Create-your-HPC-ID-account_3.png').default}
        alt="Create-your-HPC-ID-account_3"
        style={{ width: '100%', maxHeight: '300px', objectFit: 'cover', marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}
    />
1. Dont forget to submit your request! Scroll down and click on 'submit request to core'.
    <img src={require('@site/static/img/Guides/Getting-Started/Create-your-HPC-ID-account_4.png').default}
        alt="Create-your-HPC-ID-account_4"
        style={{ width: '100%', objectFit: 'cover', marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}
    />
1. You will be directed to this dashboard.
    <img src={require('@site/static/img/Guides/Getting-Started/Create-your-HPC-ID-account_5.png').default}
        alt="Create-your-HPC-ID-account_5"
        style={{ width: '100%', objectFit: 'cover', marginBottom: '1.5rem' }}
    />

:::danger Do not share your ACP account!
Just create a new account, dont share your accounts. DO IT AT YOUR OWN RISK!
1. What if another person deleted your files?
1. What if the other person copy your files, and write on your work?
:::
